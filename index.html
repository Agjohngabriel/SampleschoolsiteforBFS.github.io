Basic Foundation Schools came into existence in the 1999/2000 academic year on August 30, 1999 as Basic Foundation Nursery and Primary School. Mrs Ikodiya Jane Onuma (Director) who holds a Masters Degree in Educational Administration with several certifications and a robust experience in the educational sector, runs the schools with her team of renowned academicians. Basic Foundation Secondary Schools was later founded on Monday September 14, 2009. This brought about the incorporation of the schools as 'Basic Foundation Schools'
It is presently a center for all the external examinations like Senior School Certificate Examinations,(SSCE), National Examinations Council (NECO), Basic Education Certificate Examinations (BECE), Primary School Leaving Assessment Test (PSLAT) and State Common Entrance Examinations. The School now comprises of Crèche, Pre-nursery, Nursery, Primary, Junior and Senior Secondary Schools.
Our mission is to ensure that our students and pupils are equipped with moral and creative mind to face the challenges and realities of life We also work to empower our students with skills by exploiting their natural talents and expose them to the world of entrepreneurship.
